Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  5. Add the hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta and toss to combine. Remove from heat.
  6. Quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, tossing constantly. Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Chocolate Cake

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, oil, buttermilk, and vanilla to the flour mixture. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the hot coffee carefully. The batter will be thin, but that's correct.
  5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  8. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting once completely cooled.

Chicken Curry

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 800g chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, grated
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add chicken and brown on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add tomatoes and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Return chicken to the pot and simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.

Caesar Salad

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 large romaine lettuce heads, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shaved
  • 4 anchovy fillets (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. To make croutons: Cut bread into cubes, toss with olive oil, and bake at 375°F until golden, about 10 minutes.
  2. For the dressing, mash anchovies and garlic into a paste in a bowl.
  3. Whisk in egg yolks, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard.
  4. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking constantly until emulsified.
  5. Season dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. In a large bowl, toss romaine lettuce with dressing until well coated.
  7. Top with croutons and shaved Parmesan cheese.
  8. Serve immediately while croutons are still crispy.

Banana Bread

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 60 minutes

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  4. Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Add the flour and mix until just incorporated. Don't overmix.
  7. Fold in walnuts if using.
  8. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  10. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
